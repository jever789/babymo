iBaby Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Welcome to iBaby. We understand the importance of your personal information and are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Baby's basic info: Nickname, birth date, gender (required)
  • Baby's photos: Profile picture (optional)
  • Record data: Feeding, sleep, diaper records (required)
  • Voice input: Voice-to-text recording (optional)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, OS version
  • Advertising identifiers: For personalized ads (with your consent)

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Baby's real name, ID numbers, or sensitive identity information
  • Your precise geographic location
  • Your contacts, messages, or other unrelated information

2. Device Permissions

  • Camera: Take baby photos
  • Photo Library: Select and save photos
  • Microphone: Voice-to-text input
  • Notifications: Feeding and diaper reminders
  • App Tracking (iOS): Personalized ad display

All permissions can be disabled in system settings at any time.

3. Data Storage

All your record data is stored only on your local device. We do not upload your personal data to any server.

Important: Uninstalling the app will delete all data. Please backup regularly using the export feature.

4. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. You can purchase "Remove Ads" to disable all advertisements.

5. Children's Privacy

  • Baby data is stored locally only, never uploaded
  • We do not share baby information with third parties
  • Data is entirely under parental control

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all data in the app
  • Rectification: Modify information at any time
  • Erasure: Delete data at any time
  • Portability: Export data to universal format

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us through "Settings > Feedback" in the app.
